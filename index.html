Hi there! I'm Yang.

I'm a product designer studying Systems Design Engineering with a minor in music at the University of Waterloo. I'm passionate about holistic & empathetic product design. I was most recently a designer at Shopify on their UX Systems team, a product manager at Mattermost. I've also done research and front-end development at Manulife's innovation lab and Sunnybrook Research Institute.

In my spare time, I arrange and sing 10-part harmonies as the musical director of my a cappella group The Committee, obsess over maps, and throw stones at houses (only in the Winter, though).

Arrangement - Sing by My Chemical Romance

As Musical Director for ACE, University of Waterloo's largest and un-auditioned a cappella group, I scored an 8-part arrangement of Sing by My Chemical Romance. I wanted to create story with the arrangement, and started out with a post-apocalyptic vibe that becomes more hopeful and triumphant. I also sampled a section of Sing by The Carpenters, a song popularized by Sesame Street.
