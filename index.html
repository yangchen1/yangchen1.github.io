Hi there! I'm Yang. I'm an aspiring product designer / manager studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I'm passionate about holistic & empathetic product design. I was most recently a product manager at Mattermost, and a UX/UI designer at Boltmade and Sony. I've also done research and front-end development at Manulife RED Lab, University of Waterloo, and Sunnybrook Research Institute.

In my spare time, I arrange and score 10-part harmonies as the musical director of my a cappella group The Committee, play intramural volleyball, obsess over maps, and throw stones at houses (only in the Winter, though).

Connect with me on Twitter & LinkedIn, or drop me a line @ yang.chen.1@uwaterloo.ca. Also, feel free to check out my resume.

Thanks for visiting, and have a super day!

Arrangement - Sing by My Chemical Romance

As Musical Director for ACE, University of Waterloo's largest and un-auditioned a cappella group, I scored an 8-part arrangement of Sing by My Chemical Romance. I wanted to create story with the arrangement, and started out with a post-apocalyptic vibe that becomes more hopeful and triumphant. I also sampled a section of Sing by The Carpenters, a song popularized by Sesame Street.
